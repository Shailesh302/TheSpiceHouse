Our Story

For over three decades, The Spice House has been Mumbai's premier destination for authentic Indian cuisine, serving traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Authentic Traditions

Our master chefs use only the finest ingredients, sourcing fresh spices directly from local markets and grinding them daily to ensure every dish bursts with authentic flavors.

From our traditional tandoor oven to our time-honored cooking techniques, we preserve the essence of Indian culinary heritage while creating memorable dining experiences.

  • Fresh spices ground daily
  • Traditional tandoor cooking
  • Family recipes since 1990
  • Locally sourced ingredients

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Rajesh Kumar brings over 25 years of culinary expertise, having trained under renowned masters in Delhi and Mumbai. His passion for authentic Indian cuisine shines through every dish.

Under his guidance, our kitchen team creates dishes that honor traditional methods while ensuring consistent quality and exceptional taste that keeps our guests coming back.

"Every dish tells a story of our rich culinary heritage. We don't just serve food; we share our culture and traditions with every meal."

- Chef Rajesh Kumar
